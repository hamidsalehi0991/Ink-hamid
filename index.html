About

About the Artist

Hi, my name is Hamid. I am a tattoo artist originally from Isfahan, Iran, with over 10 years of experience in tattooing. Since 2010, I have continuously worked on refining my skills, aspiring to become an internationally recognized tattoo artist.


My artistic foundation runs deep — I hold a Master of Arts in Illustration and a Bachelor in Persian painting. The traditional Persian technique of "pardaz" — placing thousands of precise dots to build texture — trained my hand and eye to a level of detail that defines every tattoo I create.


In October 2022, I relocated to Hamburg, Germany, where I now pursue tattooing full-time. I am passionate about collaborating with creative clients and tattoo studios here in Hamburg and beyond.

"Every piece I take on is a collaboration. I want you to leave with something that feels truly yours — not just a design on skin, but a story worth telling."
— Hamid Salehi

Services

What I Offer

Every style, every size. My background in fine art means I approach each piece with composition, proportion, and meaning in mind.

Black & Gray / Realism

My specialty. Using 3RL shading techniques to create soft, textured contrast that brings depth and emotion. Portraits, nature, classical sculpture — all welcome.

Custom & Illustrative

Designed from scratch around your idea. Inspired by classical sculptures, Persian art, and symbolic elements — each piece tells your story in a unique visual language.

Cover-Ups

Transforming existing work into something new. With extensive cover-up experience, I've turned many unwanted tattoos into pieces clients are proud of. Consultation required.
